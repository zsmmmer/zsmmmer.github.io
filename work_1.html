Background

In the post-epidemic era with QR codes as the medium, how does the health code manipulate and ALIENATE people's thinking?

○Spectacular Society and QR Code Thinking

This is a speculative design project with WeChat as the object of speculation. In this project, we have conducted in-depth exploration and research on "landscape society and digital domestication", and produced a Visual Essay.

○Green Code is More Important Than Human Life?

The function of the health code is not to prove that you are healthy, but to register when and where you have been. But for the general public, the red color of the health code is a dangerous red color, which means centralized isolation for 14 days, and all expenses during the isolation period will be borne by yourself. There is a serious deviation between the design intention of the health code and the understanding of the masses, and the health code has unconsciously exacerbated the gap between people in the post-epidemic era.

○The Five-minute Life Circle in the Post-epidemic Era

For a long time, people believed that high-quality living services have always come from the city center, but the sudden outbreak of the epidemic has brought unprecedented challenges to the crowd gathering model. People suddenly find that there is a huge void of content between their home and the city service center. When epidemic prevention and control becomes the norm, even a 15-minute living circle is too big. We may need a five-minute living circle, and the community is the main body of our five-minute living circle.

Epidemic Prevention and Control

Real-time Place Monitoring and Information Disclosure
实时场所监控与信息公开

Use the health code instead of the access control card to assist the community to monitor and manage the flow of people in the closed area, and realize the disclosure of epidemic information for residents through the epidemic prevention and control window in the online program, and realize an open, transparent, efficient and convenient community epidemic situation Prevention and control platform.
