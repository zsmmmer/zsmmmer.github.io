Background

What is SUPERORGANISM? How do we discover, understand and creatively convey this concept?

SUPERORGANISM is a group of synergistic interacting organisms, with a clear division of labor between species and a strong connection by means of relationships among them.

There were several stages of exploration and experimentation to perceive and communicate this concept.

Finally, we apply simple rules of motion to create an interactive life-like system that is autonomously structured, replicated, and maintained by the organism.
